Awards...

His achievements with the bat and glove have earned him several awards, including the ICC ODI Player of the Year award twice (2008 & 2009), the Sir Garfield Sobers Trophy (ICC Cricketer of the Year) twice (2008 & 2009), the Wisden Leading Cricketer of the World award twice (2009 & 2013), and the Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna.In 2009, Dhoni became the first Indian captain to win the ICC World Cup and was awarded the Padma Shri, the fourth-highest civilian award in India, for his contribution to the sport. In 2011, Dhoni was honoured with the Padma Bhushan, the third-highest civilian award in India.He also signed off as the only skipper to win all three major ICC titles (to date). For the unversed, Dhoni received the Padma Bhushan on April 2, 2018; being the seventh anniversary of India's historic ODI World Cup win.He was awarded by a government of India by Padma Bhusan,Padma Shri and by the ICC he was honoured by ICC Men's ODI cricketer of the year in the years 2009and2008,ICC Award for Spirit of cricketin 2011 Also,ICC Men's ODI Team of the year 2014,2013,2012,2011.Dhoni received India's highest sports honour, the Major Dhyanchand Khel Ratna Award in 2008.for his outstanding achievements and the Government of India.
