Appreciations..

from P.M Modi

who retired from international cricket on August 15, took to Twitter on Thursday to thank PM Narendra Modi for appreciating his hard work on the field. PM Modi wished Dhoni the best for his future endeavours and said he hopes the former captain gets to spend more time with wife Sakshi and daughter Ziva.PM Modi also made special mention of Dhoni's love for the Armed Forces, saying he was 'most happy being among' India's army personnel.The former India captain posted pictures of a letter he received from the Prime Minister of India. In the letter, PM Modi praised MS Dhoni not only for his achievements, but also for his rise from humble beginnings."The correct way to assess your impact is as a phenomenon!" he wrote. --P.M. Modi
